About Us

Welcome to LooserPro, a startup committed to revolutionizing traffic safety through cutting-edge technology. Our mission is to provide solutions that enhance road safety and make a positive impact on communities.

Our Product: TrafficGuard

Our mobile application utilizes machine learning algorithms to detect vehicle license plates and cross-checks them with the RTO database. If a vehicle is found to have expired documents, instant notifications are sent to the application for prompt action by traffic police.
